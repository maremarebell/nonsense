Super Sentence Kit!

Our Super Sentence Kit is a tool to help students understand, speak and write sentences. The kit can be used with children of all ages.

The kit includes:

  • Simple Sentences
  • Compound Sentences
  • Relative Clauses
  • Adverbial Clauses
  • Rules for Students

integration

research & books

achievement

The Super Sentence Kit is a visual, hands-on tool designed to teach young children sentence structure and reinforce the parts of speech.

You can use the color cards any time students struggle to generate oral or written sentences. The Super Sentence Kit can be used with simple and compound sentences, relative clauses and adverbial clauses.

Practice with sentence types begins orally as that is a precursor to generating them in written form.

The Common Core State Standards Initiative has created standards for teaching sentence complexity. The Super Sentence Kit fully supports this standard.

The Super Sentence Kit is based on research from Rush University, Governor's State University, and the profoundly informative Syntax Handbook. These were instrumental in the development of the final version of the Super Sentence Kit.

In addition, for anyone interested in further reading, Don and Jenny Killgallon have developed a series of workbooks that teach detailed, complex sentences to students.

  • Sentence types are based on level of development.
  • Use with a range of students.
  • Facilitate students' production of complete sentences.
  • Assist students with expanding sentences.
  • Teach how sentences work to further enhance production and comprehension.
  • Practice sentences for conjunctions, relative clauses and adverbials.
