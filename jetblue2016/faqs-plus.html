JetBlue Plus Card Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What are the JetBlue Plus Card credit offer details?

A: This offer is subject to credit approval. Not everyone will qualify for the JetBlue Plus Card. If, at the time of your application, you do not meet the credit criteria previously established for this offer, or the income you report is insufficient based on your obligations, we may not be able to open an account for you. This offer is available to new cardmembers only. Please see Terms & Conditions for more information about rates, fees, the annual fee, costs and terms for this offer before applying.

Q: What is the annual fee for the JetBlue Plus Card?

A: There is a $99 annual fee for the JetBlue Plus Card.

Q: How do I earn TrueBlue points?

A: With your JetBlue Card, you earn 6 TrueBlue points for every $1 spent on purchases made directly with JetBlue Airways, 2 points for every $1 spent on restaurant and grocery store purchases as defined by the Merchant Category Code and 1 point for every $1 spent on all other purchases. Please note that your total purchases include purchases made by you and your authorized users, and that returns and refunds will be deducted from the total. Barclaycard is not responsible for incorrectly coded purchases.

Q: Is there a limit to the number of points I can earn?

A: No. There is no limit to the total number of points you can earn with your JetBlue Plus Card, as long as the program continues and your account remains open, active and in good standing.

Q: How many bonus points will I earn for activating and using the card in the first 90 days?

A: A limited time bonus offer will allow you to earn 30,000 bonus points after making $1,000 in purchases (that are not later returned or rescinded) with your JetBlue Plus Card within 90 days of opening your credit card account . This one-time offer is valid for first-time cardmembers with new accounts only. Your bonus points will be deposited into your TrueBlue account 4-6 weeks after your qualifying transactions have posted.

Q: Will I earn bonus points on my account anniversary?

A: Yes. On your JetBlue Plus Card account anniversary, we’ll award 5,000 bonus points to your TrueBlue account – as long as your credit card account remains open, active and in good standing. Please allow 4-6 weeks for bonus points to be deposited into your TrueBlue account after your anniversary date.

Q: How do I redeem points for Award Flights?

A: Once you have enough TrueBlue points for an Award Flight, redeeming is easy. Visit jetblue.com/trueblue and sign in to your TrueBlue account. Click on “Book Travel.” Then, select the option to search using points.

The number of Points required for an Award Flight is determined by JetBlue in its sole discretion based on the published base fare at the time of booking, which depends on, among other things, the flight chosen and applicability of peak travel periods. The number of Points required for a particular flight may change at any time until the booking is confirmed and Points are applied as payment for the Award Flight. Taxes and fees apply to award flights, are the responsibility of the passenger, and will vary based on your departure and destination cities. See https://trueblue.jetblue.com/web/trueblue/how-it-works-using-points for full details on Award Flights.

Q: Do my TrueBlue points expire?

A: Points earned and deposited into your TrueBlue account don’t expire! However, should a member close his or her TrueBlue account, the points in that account will be terminated. TrueBlue program Terms & Conditions apply.

Q: Are there any blackout dates for Award Flights?

A: No! Redeem for Award Flights with no blackout dates on JetBlue-operated flights.

Q: Is my first checked bag free on JetBlue flights?

A: Yes. You and up to 3 travel companions on the same reservation, who have purchased Blue fares, will each receive their first checked bag free on JetBlue-operated flights that do not include first checked bag free. All ticket purchases must be made with your JetBlue Plus Card, and the primary cardmember’s TrueBlue member number must be entered at the time of booking. Applicable overweight and excess baggage fees apply.

Q: How do I earn Mosaic?

A: You’ll qualify for Mosaic after you make $50,000 in net purchases that post to your January-December billing statements. Purchases made by your authorized users also count toward your qualifying spend. Please allow 4-6 weeks for Mosaic after you’ve qualified. Once qualified, you’ll be eligible for Mosaic for the remainder of the current calendar year and the following calendar year – as long as your credit card account remains open, active and in good standing.

Please see https://trueblue.jetblue.com/web/trueblue/mosaic details regarding Mosaic benefits and terms.

Q: How do I earn 10% of my points back?

A: You’ll earn 10% of your points back to use toward your next redemption every time you redeem. For example, if you redeem 25,000 points for a JetBlue flight, you’ll receive 2,500 points as a bonus to use toward your next redemption. Points typically post to your TrueBlue account within 4-6 weeks after your redemption is processed.

Q: What does no foreign transaction fees mean?

A: No foreign transaction fees means you don’t pay additional transaction fees on purchases you make while travelling internationally.

Q: How can I receive a $100 statement credit on a Getaways Vacation Package purchase?

A: For each calendar year that you purchase a JetBlue Getaways vacation package of $100 or more with your JetBlue Plus Card at jetblue.com or 1-800-JETBLUE, you’ll receive a $100 statement credit – as long as your credit card account remains open, active and in good standing. Your savings will appear as a statement credit 6-8 weeks after your transaction posts to your account.

Q: Can I save on inflight purchases?

A: Yes. You'll qualify for a 50% savings on cocktails, food and other eligible purchases. Sorry, but wireless internet access and dutyfree purchases do not qualify. Your savings will appear as a statement credit 4-6 weeks after your transaction posts to your account.

Q: What is a chip card?

A: In addition to storing your account information on a magnetic stripe on the back of your card, chip cards also keep your account information on a small electronic chip. Since the chip uses encryption technology, your transaction information is very secure, and your card is almost impossible to reproduce. The JetBlue Plus Card is a chip-and-signature card with PIN capability for use at unattended terminals – offering U.S. cardmembers optimal acceptance and convenience when traveling abroad.

Q: What is $0 Fraud Liability protection?

A: This means that you are not responsible for any unauthorized transactions made with your account that you report to us in a timely manner.
